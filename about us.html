Our Story

At Mastermind Daycare, we've been a trusted partner for families since 2006, offering a safe and stimulating environment for children to learn and grow. Our passion for early childhood education drives us to create experiences that spark curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child we care for.

We're not just a daycare – we're a family committed to nurturing the whole child through age-appropriate activities that foster emotional, social, and cognitive development. With a focus on play-based learning, we ensure that each child is prepared for the next stage of their educational journey.

Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a loving, secure, and enriching environment where children can thrive through play, discovery, and meaningful learning experiences. We are dedicated to supporting the holistic growth of each child while partnering with families to foster strong foundations for the future.

Vision Statement

To be a community where children feel safe, valued, and inspired to explore the world around them. We strive to be recognized as a leader in early childhood education, preparing children for lifelong learning through a caring, inclusive, and innovative approach.

Core Values

  • Nurturing Environment: We create a loving and supportive space where children feel safe and valued.
  • Holistic Growth: We focus on developing the whole child - emotionally, socially, and cognitively.
  • Creativity and Curiosity: We encourage exploration, creativity, and problem-solving through hands-on learning.
  • Family Partnership: We work hand-in-hand with families to support each child's growth and development.
  • Inclusive Community: We embrace diversity and teach children the importance of respect, kindness, and empathy.

Meet The Team

At Mastermind Daycare, we believe that great caregivers make all the difference. Our team is comprised of highly qualified educators who are passionate about early childhood development. With backgrounds in early education and child psychology, our staff is dedicated to creating a nurturing and supportive environment for every child.
